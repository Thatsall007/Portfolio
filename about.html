About Me

I am an AI and Machine Learning enthusiast with a strong foundation in data science, analytics, and problem-solving. My journey in technology started with a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering and has continued with a Master's degree in Applied Mechanics at IIT Madras, where I have been able to deepen my understanding of advanced computational techniques and their applications in real-world challenges. My academic journey, coupled with securing an All-India Rank of 848 in GATE 2023, reflects my dedication and passion for continuous learning and growth.

Throughout my educational and professional experiences, I have been involved in cutting-edge projects such as using Fourier Neural Operators to predict the mechanical properties of composite materials, time series analysis for air pollution forecasting, and building AI-powered solutions like chatbots and sentiment analysis models. These projects reflect my commitment to integrating AI and ML into practical domains, aiming for solutions that push technological boundaries.

In addition to my academic and professional pursuits, I have developed a keen interest in the fast-evolving field of Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs). I enjoy reading blogs to stay updated with the latest trends and research, and I regularly contribute my insights by writing on Medium, where I share my learnings and experiences in AI, ML, and LLMs. Writing and reading blogs helps me consolidate my knowledge while staying connected with the AI community. With a curious and collaborative mindset, I seek to contribute to the ever-growing AI ecosystem, solving complex problems and creating impactful solutions.

My goal is to continue exploring innovative applications in AI and to be part of shaping the future of generative technologies and intelligent systems.
